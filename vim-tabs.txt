This Are Commands For Working In Multiple Tabs In VIM Editor .

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

:tabnew <File_Name> => Creating A New Tab With New File Name . You Can Only Enter ':tabnew' For Only Creating A New Tab .

:tabclose OR :tabc OR :q => Closing Current Tab .

:tabonly : Close All Tabs Except Current .

:tabnext OR :tabn => Switching To Next Tab .

:tabprevious OR :tabp OR :tabN => Switching To Previous Tab .

:tabfirst => Switching To First Tab .

:tablast OR :tabl => Switching To Last Tab .

:tabfind OR :tabf => Find a Tab .

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
